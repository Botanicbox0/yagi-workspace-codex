# ADR 0005 — AI Assist Requires Human Approval

Status: Accepted

## Context

AI can help review briefs, organize references, and generate drafts. But YAGI Workspace records client/studio agreements.

## Decision

AI output is always a suggestion or draft until accepted by a human.

## Consequences

- AI suggestions have explicit lifecycle.
- Official board content is human-approved.
- AI does not approve deliverables or contest winners.
- Usage is logged for cost and audit.
