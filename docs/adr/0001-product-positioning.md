# ADR 0001 — Product Positioning

Status: Accepted

## Context

YAGI Workspace could drift into one of several categories:
- generic project management tool
- AI generation tool
- creator marketplace
- public contest platform

## Decision

YAGI Workspace is a private studio operating system with a contest layer.

It is not primarily a marketplace and not primarily an AI generation tool.

## Consequences

- Private workspace collaboration remains the core.
- Contest exists as YAGI-operated campaign infrastructure.
- Creator profiles support contest participation and future ecosystem growth.
- AI is assistive and human-approved.
